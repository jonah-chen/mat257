    As $|f(x)|\leq|x|^2$ for all $x$, $|f(0)|\leq 0$ hence $f(0)=0$. Then,
    \begin{align*}
        \lim_{h\to 0}\frac{|f(h)-f(0)-0h|}{|h|}=\lim_{h\to 0}\frac{|f(h)|}{|h|}\leq \lim_{h\to 0}\frac{|h|^2}{|h|}=0
    \end{align*}
    Thus, $f$ is differentiable at $0$ and the derivative is $0$.
